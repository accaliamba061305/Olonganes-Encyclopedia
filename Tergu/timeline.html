The Timeline of Tergu

As you can see, this is a brief timeline of Tergu.

I. The Sultimathischnorisis Eon (7 - 4.15 billion years ago)
  • Kimivichen Era (7 - 5.34 billion years ago)
    • Orizhinik Period (7 - 6.2 billion years ago)
    • Strivichinik Period (6.2 - 5.34 billion years ago) The first period of micro-biological species.
  • Alikhopen Era (5.34 - 4.92 billion years ago)
  • Blavaleshtien Era (4.92 - 4.21 billion years ago)
  • Ondinungstarzen Era (4.21 - 4.15 billion years ago) This era was brought to an abrupt end by the collision of three giant meteorites with Tergu, causing mass extinction.
II. The Reskoverikasis Eon (4.15 - 2.8 billion years ago)

aka The Recovery Eon

  • Prè-Reskoveriken (4.15 - 4 billion years ago)
  • Prosoreskoveriken (4 - 3.5 billion years ago)
  • Manereskoveriken (3.5 - 2.8 billion years ago) Ended due to plate divergence precipitated by radical thermal shifts in Tergu’s core.
III. The Kelephorosis Eon (2.8 billion - 30 million years ago)
  • Balestien (2.8 - 1.98 billion years ago)
  • Balestatien (1.98 - 1.2 billion years ago)
  • Mongerustiyen (1.2 billion - 900 million years ago)
  • Groronzhen (900 - 499 million years ago)
  • Magorovichliniyen (499 - 30 million years ago) Formation of a new supercontinent. The "Stedetimizhe" (Ice Age IV) marked the end of this eon.
IV. The Concugentasis Eon (30 million years ago - Present)
  • Allegor-Tumbastimen Era: (30 million years ago - IIII) (IIII means the present.)
